About the EcoPrompt
Abstract

This project aims to significantly improve the efficiency and effectiveness of image generation tasks performed by Large Language Models (LLMs) while concurrently enhancing memory and processing efficiency. This improvement is achieved through a synergistic blend of Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques and Knowledge Graph strategies. The core objective is to transform user prompts into more contextually relevant and informative inputs, resulting in both higher-quality generated images and optimized resource utilization. Traditionally, image generation with LLMs, such as Stable Diffusion or similar models, relies heavily on the accuracy and specificity of user-provided prompts. However, users often struggle to articulate precise and context-rich prompts, leading to suboptimal or undesired results and consuming excessive computational resources, which increases the Carbon Footprints produced by the LLM. Through the project's innovative framework, users will benefit from automated prompt enhancements that refine and contextualize their instructions. The project's ambition extends to optimizing memory and processing efficiency. By minimizing the need for exhaustive iterations and reducing computational overhead, it not only caters to user needs but also aligns with sustainability objectives in AI research.

Eliminating Bias

Removing Bias in Generated Images

Optimized prompts in image generation effectively counter biases linked to distinct subject properties. The language model, guided by refined user prompts, can creatively reinterpret and reshape conventional characteristics of both Subject 1 and Subject 2, fostering diversity. Particularly in unconventional scenarios, where subjects interchange traits, optimized prompts play a crucial role in guiding the model through unique conceptualizations. This process not only improves visual quality but also encourages the model to navigate unconventional subject properties, fostering a more inclusive and creatively challenging narrative.

GPU Cycle Reduction

In the realm of image generation, where computational power is pivotal, an analysis of mean energy consumption between User prompts and Ecoprompts reveals a significant divergence. The evaluation methodology, executed through Wandb, involved optimizing diverse prompts using a prompt generation model. The results demonstrate the advantageous impact of Ecoprompts, showcasing a reduction in both GPU utilization and energy consumption. This crucial insight is organized into an Excel file, with representing the comparative analysis. On average, Ecoprompts lead to noticeably lower energy consumption in the computationally intensive scenario, underscoring the eco-friendly design principles embedded within Ecoprompts. By minimizing GPU workload and overall energy usage, Ecoprompts not only contribute to energy efficiency but also align with sustainable computing practices.
